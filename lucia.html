Lucia

Lucia (pr. LOO-shuh) is a philosopher and opinionated neer-do-well currently dwelling in Milwaukee, WI. They like to write songs and take photos. Since Fuck The Internet is their personal blog, many of the articles on the site are written from their perspective.

Background

I was born in Missouri and lived there until moving to Wisconsin when I was 12. I had the sort of upbringing that makes you a sort of interesting person but ultimately alienates you from your peers. I know a lot of people have had a similar experience, and we're all interesting and cool and fucked up in our unique ways. Apart from a year or two in montessouri elementary school, I was homeschooled until 8th grade. For most of our childhood my siblings and I were technically "unschooled," which is one way to say I spent most of my early years running around in the woods instead of learning math. That's not to say my childhood was devoid of learning. I read constantly, and spent countless days at the library while my parents worked at their restaurant.
