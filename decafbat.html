Decafbat

Decafbat is an illustrator and philosopher[]. Her natural habitats are Furaffinity and Tumblr. She is part of a writing and illustrating duo currently creating the comic Larvie and Libro.

Early Life

Decafbat is a mysterious creature of unknown origin. As far as I know, she existed long before I was born and will continue to live on long after I'm gone.

Career

Although she had been drawing since she was just a sprout, she began sharing her art on the internet when she was in her tween/early teen years. Most of her art is considered lost media

Lost Media

"In which Nicolas Cage battles Guy Fieri to the death." was a macromedia flash animation created by Decafbat in the late 2010s and hosted on youtube.com. She created it using a method that is commonly known as photobashing today. By combining images and digital painting and animating them by moving elements on keyframes. It was set to dramatic music and told the tale of Nicolas Cage's character from Con Air getting into an epic battle to the death with famed TV personality and restauranteur Guy Fieri.
In late 2025, it was removed from Youtube for violating terms of service, and was sadly never archived. Thankfully, the companion comic, "Con Air: Choose Your Own Adventure" is still hosted on MSPFA.com and features many of the same artistic stylings. Unfortunately, it seems that Poe's epic battle may forever be lost to time.
